Apoth3osis Audit Procedure 1.0

Purpose. Define a fair, confidential, and efficient audit process for verifying usage and determining usage‑based fees.

1. Frequency & Notice. No more than once every 12 months. Licensor provides at least fifteen (15) business days’ written notice.

2. Independent Auditor. Licensor will engage an independent third‑party auditor under NDA. Auditor will conduct the audit and issue a report to both parties.

3. Scope. Auditor may review records reasonably necessary to determine usage of the Software, including counts, runtime metrics, and distribution metadata. Auditor will not access end‑user PII beyond what is necessary for verification.

4. Confidentiality. All materials reviewed and the audit report are Confidential Information.

5. Method. Auditor may sample representative periods and extrapolate in good faith if full data is not available. You will cooperate reasonably and provide APIs or exports if available.

6. Costs. Licensor pays audit costs unless underpayment exceeds five percent (5%) for the audited period, in which case You pay audit costs.

7. True‑Up & Dispute. Any underpayment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice. Disputes are resolved per CECL arbitration.
