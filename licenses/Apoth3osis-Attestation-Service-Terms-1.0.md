Apoth3osis Attestation Service Terms 1.0

1. Scope. Licensor provides attestation services for certified builds of the Software, including provenance (e.g., SLSA), SBOM, build metadata, signatures, and transparency log references.

2. Deliverables. For each certified release: (a) attestation bundle, (b) signature and verification instructions, (c) transparency entry ID/URL. Licensor may update formats over time while preserving verifiability.

3. SLA (summary). Reasonable commercial efforts to: (a) publish attestations within 3 business days of release, (b) remedy broken verification within 5 business days, (c) notify subscribers of revocations.

4. Revocation. Licensor may revoke an attestation upon discovery of material error or compromise. Licensor will publish a revocation entry and notify subscribers.

5. Confidentiality. Attestation bundles and associated private build metadata are Confidential Information under CECL.

6. Verification Tools. Licensor provides a reference CLI and public keys. Verification is provided “AS IS”.

7. Suspension/Termination. For nonpayment or material breach, Licensor may suspend services and revoke right to claim certification; reinstatement upon cure.

8. Fees. Included in CECL fees unless otherwise specified.
